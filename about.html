ABOUT INK ON BTC

Ink, a highly anticipated PFP collection, is all set to launch on the Bitcoin blockchain. It is not just a collection but a community, with personalized and hand-drawn art created by Joey Dodd. This project, which began on the 2nd of January, 2024, is driven by the motto "Ink is For The People," emphasizing the collective spirit that underpins this unique venture. The team recently announced that the mint price for this project will be 0.0058 BTC and launched on Magic Eden, wherein 10,000 supplies will be minted. lorem30
