Ink's Journey

January 2, 2024

The start of the Journey

Ink on BTC posted their first ever post on twitter which contains a video of Joey showcasing his incredible skills by making the custom INK piece of Brunno.

January 4, 2024

Opening of Doors to the People

Frenchie welcomed the people with an announcement as Ink opened their discord channel to the public.

January 7, 2024

Trendsetter

People of Ink showed their utmost support for the project, and it became trending on Twitter with the hashtag '#getINKed.' People let their support for Ink be known worldwide.

January 12, 2024

First Chapter

The first page of Ink's comics is published revealing the main goal of the project, inking the people. Ink is for the people.

January 17 and 19, 2024

Inking the Anthem

Ink's anthem was created during a live event hosted by Mhelodius, showing the people how he's been making the team's music for the project.

January 21, 2024

New Chapter Unlocked

Another milestone reached! 20k twitter followers on twitter and Brunno hosted an Ink giveaway for the first Phase of the project. Page two of comics unveiled.

January 23, 2024

Ink is For the People

Phase two of the project is announced composing of 10K collection of generative arts. Fuck the influenzas, INK IS FOR THE PEOPLE.

January 27, 2024

Chapter Three

As the story continues, Wades, together with Bee and Min, tried to escape the third party scums.

February 1, 2024

Emmy got Inked

Ink announced that the phase two collection (10K) will be launched on Magic Eden launchpad. Once again, people of Ink caused a buzz on Twitter, which made Ink trending for the second time.

February 2, 2024

All Aboard

Ink's 10K application form has been launched; it allows you to form a four-member squad, making it easier for an individual to get an allocation spot since you are collaborating with other people.

February 17, 2024

What Comes First?

Steven announced that the phase two collection would be launched before the phase one collection. He also stated that the phase one collection will be a slow burn as they put it on the chain by the time they finish it.

February 18, 2024

Story Continues

As the trio fight their way out, Min got left behind and showcased his power that came from his dragon ink. Bee and Wades escaped.

February 19, 2024

Expanding the Knowledge on Ordinals

The team of Ink, together with Magic Eden, hosted an event that aims to expand the knowledge and educate the people about ordinals.

February 21, 2024

Story Got Inked

Another gathering took place in Ink's Discord channel. A group of Ink members led a live comic creation session, enabling participants to influence the direction of the story through active involvement in the event.

February 22, 2024

Beginning of History

Ink began inscribing the Phase One collection, becoming the first ever Legacy Sats collection.

February 23, 2024

#getDINKed

Frenchie began getting rid of alts/bots/suspicious accounts which exposed a lot of people. Ink will always be for the people! On the same day, Comics, Ink-Custom-Gallery, and X-tweets channels have opened up on discord.

February 24, 2024

50k People

InkOnBTC Twitter account reached 50 thousand followers in less than two months.

February 27, 2024

Marked Legacy

Ink is the first ever legacy sats collection and will definitely leave a mark in Ordinals history.

March 2, 2024

Security Breached!

Zetman's twitter account got compromised which caused spreading malicious of links to his followers. On the same day, Ink acquired Bitcoin Shroom #7. FUD!

March 8, 2024

Hold Up! Wait a Minute

Ink announced that the phase two (10K) collection will be having a mint price of 0.0058 BTC. INK IS THE PEOPLE! On the same day, Magic Eden officially listed the Legacy Sats on their platform.

March 9, 2024

Binance

Ink on BTC was featured on Binance news, highlighting its mint price and total supply.

March 9, 2024

Mourning

On the same day, Frenchie's dog, the inspiration for his name, crosses the rainbow bridge.

March 12, 2024

Clap Back

People whose accounts got banned on Ink's discord caused a lot of drama, causing more flames to a burning fire. Frenchie and the team started dropping receipts to expose the people caught doing these unfair actions.

March 14, 2024

Flag of Ink

Blade of God created an in game flag that is specially made for Ink. The said flag can be redeemed by using the gift code "Ink".

March 16, 2024

Explosion of Talent

This is a night to remember to many people in the channel. Every participants did not disappoint exhibiting their talent on the event named "Ink Got Talent". Ink is full of talented people.

March 23, 2024

LONG WAIT IS OVER

Finally, the team announced the mint date of the project. The project will begin minting on 28th of March, year 2024.
